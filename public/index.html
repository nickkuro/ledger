¶ LEDGER

A private note-keeping app for characters and stories, built with Discord login so your group can each have their own space.

How it works
Login
You sign in with Discord. The app asks Discord who you are, then creates a personal space just for your account. No passwords stored.
Characters
Create named characters with a color. Each character gets their own set of notes. Switch between them using the selector at the top of the sidebar. "All notes" shows everything grouped by character.
Notes
Write in plain text or Markdown. Notes auto-save as you type. After a short pause, the text renders formatted — click it to go back to editing.
Tags
Add tags to notes from the editor. Tags appear in the sidebar as filters you can click to narrow the list.
What's stored
On the server
Your Discord user ID, username, and avatar URL. All your characters and notes (title, body, tags, timestamps). Session files so you stay logged in across restarts.
In your browser
Your theme preference (dark/light) in localStorage. A session cookie that identifies your login — nothing else.
What's not stored
No analytics, no tracking, no ads. Nothing is shared with Discord beyond the initial login handshake.
Built with Node.js, Express, and Discord OAuth2. Created with AI assistance.
